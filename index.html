Using Archive Viewer
Pick The Publication

Clicking The Longview Button At The Top For Example Will Take You To That Dedicated Viewer With Years To Choose From.

Navigating The Viewer

You can use the arrows at the top of the calendar to navigate throught the months and years.

Viewing Archives

Click on the day on the calendar you would like to see archives from and they will appear below the calendar with the corresponding date where you can view to your hearts content.

Options

When Viewing the PDF's you have plenty of options like download, presentation mode, print, rotation and scrolling settings, zoom, and a page selection sidebar.
